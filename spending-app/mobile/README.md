# Ranceipt

**Smart Receipt Management & Spending Goals**

Take control of your finances with intelligent receipt tracking and personalized spending goals.

---

## The Idea

Ranceipt transforms the way you manage your spending. Instead of manually categorizing receipts or losing track of where your money goes, our app intelligently processes your receipts and helps you stay within your personal spending goals.

Whether you're looking to budget better, understand your spending patterns, or achieve specific financial targets, Ranceipt makes it effortless.

---

## Key Features

- **Smart Receipt Recognition** - Capture receipts with your camera and let AI handle the categorization
- **Automatic Categorization** - Receipts are automatically organized into spending categories
- **Personal Spending Goals** - Set and track spending targets for different categories
- **Transaction Overview** - View all your transactions in one place with clear insights
- **Bank Integration** - Connect with your Bunq account for seamless transaction syncing
- **Custom Categories** - Create your own spending categories tailored to your lifestyle
